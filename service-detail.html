General Cleans

Cleaning tasks will be different for weekly and monthly cleans. We are more than happy to alter the rooms cleaned each time or focus on certain areas more. Any special requests are also welcomed if discussed before the booking and agreed by both parties. If you would like us to use your cleaning products, that's fine - we can do that too. You might find some useful information in one of our blog posts about regular cleans.

  • Dusting down and hand wiping all the surfaces throughout
  • Dusting down skirting boards (also known as kick or base boards) and light fittings
  • Tidy up as much as possible
  • Vacuum sofas and other soft furniture
  • Give a light clean to the dishwasher, washing machine, microwave
  • Cleaning of internal windows and mirrors, glass surfaces
  • Disinfecting all surfaces in the kitchen
  • Making sure no bread crumbs are left (empty the toaster)
